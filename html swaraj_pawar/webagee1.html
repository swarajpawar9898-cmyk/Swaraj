ABOUT THE JARVIS

Jarvis is an advanced artificial intelligence system shown in the Marvel movies. It was created by Tony Stark to assist him in his daily work and superhero activities. Jarvis can control computers, manage data, operate Iron Man suits, and even help in making important decisions. It understands voice commands and responds intelligently. Jarvis is known for being smart, fast, and loyal to Tony Stark. In later movies, Jarvis evolves into Vision, becoming more powerful and human-like Iron Man is a famous superhero from Marvel whose real name is Tony Stark. He is a brilliant scientist and billionaire who creates a powerful armored suit using advanced technology. With this suit, he can fly, shoot energy beams, and protect the world from dangerous enemies. Although he is confident and sometimes arrogant, he has a kind heart and always works to save people. Iron Man became very popular after the 2008 movie and is one of the main members of the Avengers..
